Sankranthi Celebrations

Every year on behalf of DOST we celebrate Sankranthi as a community festival in Duggirala college grounds. People come from all over and enjoy the celebration. This time also, DOST is planning to organize Mega Medical camp on 11th Jan and Sankranthi celebrations on 13th and 14th Jan.

Schedule and program details

In our village, we celebrate Sankranthi with the active participation of the majority of villagers, individuals from different regions across the country, and Non-Resident Indians (NRIs).

Medical Camp (Day 1)

Organized a medical camp at Jampala Vari Kalyana Mandapam on 01/12/2024. A healthcare team from Manipal Super Speciality Hospital provided health services in General Medicine, Orthopedics, and Oncology specialties.
